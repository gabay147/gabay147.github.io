Hello! I am Jon Gabay...

... and I'm a 3D modeling and rigging artist in northeast Kansas! I am passionate about creating unique characters for both games and animation, and I love creating the props and scenery to help tell their stories!

I work primarily in Autodesk Maya, ZBrush, and Substance Painter, and I earned my AAS in Animation for Entertainment and Game Art Design in May 2021 from Johnson County Community College.
